About AI Based Question Generation System

An intelligent system that leverages AI to generate high-quality questions from uploaded documents.

Our Mission

The AI Based Question Generation System was created to simplify the process of creating educational assessments, quizzes, and study materials. Our mission is to help educators, students, and professionals save time while creating high-quality questions from their learning materials.

By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and natural language processing, we can automatically generate relevant questions that test understanding and knowledge of any subject matter contained in your documents.

Key Benefits

  • Save Time - Generate questions in seconds instead of hours
  • AI-Powered - Leverages advanced NLP models for quality questions
  • Multiple Formats - Create both multiple-choice and structured questions
  • Customizable - Edit and refine generated questions
  • Easy Export - Download questions and answers as PDF

How It Works

1. Upload

Upload your PDF or Word document containing the content you want to generate questions from.

2. Configure

Choose the number of questions, question types, and difficulty level.

3. Generate

Our AI analyzes your document and generates relevant questions and answers.

4. Export

Review, edit, and export your questions and answers to PDF.

Technology Behind the System

AI Based Question Generation System uses state-of-the-art natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning technologies to analyze documents and generate meaningful questions. Our system leverages advanced AI APIs that have been trained on vast amounts of text data to understand context, identify key concepts, and formulate relevant questions.

Document Processing

We use advanced text extraction techniques to process PDF and Word documents, ensuring we capture all the important content for question generation.

Question Generation

Our AI models identify key sentences and concepts in the text and transform them into questions using sophisticated natural language generation techniques.

Answer Generation

For each question, our system determines the correct answer and, for multiple-choice questions, generates plausible distractors to challenge the test-taker.
